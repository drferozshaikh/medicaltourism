Your Treatment Journey in Mumbai

We guide you at every step — from initial consultation to recovery and follow-up.

Step-by-Step Process

1. Share Medical Reports

Send your reports and medical history through WhatsApp or email for initial review.

2. Doctor Review & Opinion

Specialists evaluate your case and provide treatment options and estimated costs.

3. Travel Planning

We assist with visa guidance, travel planning, and scheduling your hospital visit.

4. Arrival in Mumbai

Airport pickup is arranged and you are guided to your hotel or hospital.

5. Consultation & Treatment

Meet specialists, undergo procedures, and receive hospital care.

6. Recovery & Stay

Post-treatment recovery with support for accommodation and follow-ups.

7. Return & Follow-up

After returning home, we stay connected for reports and doctor follow-up.
