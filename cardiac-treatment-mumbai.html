Heart Treatment in Mumbai India

Mumbai is one of the leading destinations for cardiac care in India, offering advanced procedures at significantly lower costs compared to other countries. Patients from Africa, the Middle East, and international regions travel to Mumbai for safe and effective heart treatments.

Cardiac Procedures Available

Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery (CABG)

Open-heart surgery to improve blood flow to the heart.

Angioplasty & Stent Placement

Minimally invasive procedure to open blocked arteries.

Heart Valve Replacement

Surgical repair or replacement of damaged heart valves.

Pacemaker Implantation

Device placement to regulate heart rhythm.
